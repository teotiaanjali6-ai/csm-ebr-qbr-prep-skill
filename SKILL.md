# EBR / QBR Deck Preparation Skill

## When to Use
Activate when a user asks for help with:
- Preparing an Executive Business Review (EBR)
- Preparing a Quarterly Business Review (QBR)
- Structuring a customer review presentation
- Summarizing customer usage or performance
- Preparing slides for customer meetings

## Instructions

When activated:

1. Ask the user for key inputs if they are missing:
   - Customer name
   - Time period covered
   - Customer goals
   - Product usage data
   - Adoption metrics
   - Support ticket insights
   - Major wins
   - Risks or challenges
   - Upcoming roadmap items

2. Organize the EBR/QBR into sections:

Recommended structure:

1. Executive Summary
2. Customer Goals & Success Metrics
3. Product Adoption & Usage Trends
4. Key Achievements
5. Business Impact / ROI
6. Support & Operational Insights
7. Risks & Mitigation
8. Expansion Opportunities
9. Roadmap & Next Steps

3. Generate:
- Slide titles
- Talking points
- Data insights
- Executive-friendly language

4. Ensure the tone is strategic and outcome-focused.

## Output Format

Provide:

- Slide deck
- Talking points
- Research notes
